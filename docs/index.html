Klackit provides free mobile phone charging kiosks for venues to accommodate their patrons, assisting in the comfort and longevity of their stay. The kiosks provide an out-of-home advertising (OOH) platform for advertisers to engage their target audience by time and location via Broadsign's API.

I was responsible for developing the back-end handling all requests to/from kiosks, the portal and the chat console. The back-end handles everything from creating new locker sessions, relaying chat messages, administrating users, alerting Klackit when a kiosk unexpectedly goes offline, retrieving historical telemetry data and handling archived data by offline kiosks.

In addition to the back-end, I also designed and developed both the front-end applications for the portal and the chat console. The portal provides an intuitive interface for Klackit's staff to manage all kiosks provisioned within the system, the chat console is used to handle support requests where an agent can quickly and decisively resolve customer requests.

The system was deployed to production in February 2023. As of April 2023, the new system has serviced over 1000 customers and is active in over 100 venues.
